NoteEz

Gain skills with practice

Haven't we all faced the dilemma of choosing notes before exam? Which teacher has best notes, whom to refer for best clarity, which is good to practice numericals?

To solve this problem, we have designed this website which curates each note from all the faculties here at VIT and then provides the best of them all, ranked by the students themselves. Students may further filter the notes based on their preference and upload their notes for the juniors to refer.
